## 💼 Experience / 工作經歷

**2016 – Present　洪天創藝事業有限公司　Co-Founder / 共同創辦人**
Providing industrial design, service design, product planning, packaging, branding, and international award consulting. Leading strategic projects in service transformation, brand upgrade, and organizational redesign. Built **kidddea**, an early childhood education brand.

提供工業設計 / 服務設計 / 產品企劃 / 包裝設計 / 品牌整合規劃 / 國際競賽顧問等服務，協助客戶服務轉型、品牌升級、組織再設計等策略型專案，並建構學齡前兒童啟蒙教育品牌 **kidddea**。

---

**2014 – 2016　同樂工作室　Brand Manager / 品牌經理**
Founded independent brand **BLACK TAILORS**, specializing in natural mineral accessories. Expanded brand presence through domestic and international exhibitions.

創立 **BLACK TAILORS** 獨立設計品牌，以天然礦石材質製作礦物系配件為品牌核心，透過國內外參展拓展品牌市場。

---

**2013 – 2014　富奇想 / Foxconn Group　Founding Member & Senior Front-End Design Researcher / 創始團隊成員・資深前端設計研究員**
Selected as one of five founding members stationed at Foxconn. Developed the "Wall Economy" design ecosystem under the tablet business unit, focusing on front-end research of user contexts and behaviors.

由奇想挑選五名創始成員進駐鴻海，在平板事業群下發展「牆經濟」設計生態系，專注於前端設計研究使用者情境與行為。

---

**2011 – 2013　奇想創造事業有限公司　Intelligence Center / Front-End Design Researcher / 情報中心・前端設計研究員**
Led front-end design research for client projects; managed the Qi-Xiang design media platform and published design trend articles; developed and co-facilitated Design Thinking workshop curricula for corporate training.

負責設計專案之前端研究；奇想抱報網站架構與自媒體運營，發布設計趨勢文章；設計思考工作坊之企業內訓課程內容制定與協同帶隊。
